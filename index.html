Our Story

Every Home Begins
with a Story

Every home begins as an empty space. But an empty house is not a home until it is filled with comfort, warmth, and memories. At UrbanCraft, furniture is never just wood, steel, or fabric — it becomes part of everyday life.

It is where children grow. Families gather. Friends laugh. Dreams begin. Memories are created.

About Us

From My Father's Workshop to Your Home

Hello, I'm Harish, founder of UrbanCraft Furniture. My journey began in my father's workshop, watching him transform raw wood into furniture that became part of people's everyday lives.

He never believed furniture was just a business. He believed every piece should make someone's life more comfortable. That philosophy shaped who I am — and it shapes UrbanCraft today.
